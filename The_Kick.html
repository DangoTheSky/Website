The Kick


The Kick is a short animation featuring two humanoid figures. One figure is sneaking up to the other figure before initiating a kick that sent the other figure to space. This animation is a prototype that is submitted for an art project, where this project was meant to learn basic animation in Blender such as creating a human figure using mesh modelling, keyframing, posing, and positing of the extra elements. Though Daniel has some previous experience with Blender, it was only mainly modelling and sculpting.
