Artist Bio


Daniel Ounalom is a Media Arts student and artist who focuses on building the skills and practices in his line of work to pursue a career creating worlds that people can live in without physically residing in. Whether creating video games inside of Unity or creating short animations inside of Blender, Daniel wants the audience to question and critique societal issues in our present reality while not breaking the immersion that the audience is experiencing inside his narrative-driven worlds.
