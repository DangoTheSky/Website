Artist Statement

Learning new skills and creating new art forms using these skills drives my practices as an artist. This process helps me establish who I am as an artist and what style defines my practices. At the end of this process, I could pursue my goal of creating the worlds I always wanted to make.

I always ask myself, "Does the Audience understand my message in my worlds, and what areas should I improve to achieve my end goal." These questions should be able to keep me trying to improve my processes and practices regarding what kind of artist I am.

Currently, I am expanding my skills and knowledge in two distinct mediums: Unity and Blender. Inside Unity, I want to use the software to create interactive digital worlds that allow people to question both issues without breaking the immersion inside these games. Inside Blender, I want to use the software to develop narratives following a character that would also ask both the issues in the animation and reality.
